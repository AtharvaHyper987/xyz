MY LEAST FAVORITE FOOD

My least favorite food is omelet. It is because it tastes bad for me and I dont really like it the way it tastes. I ate omelet a few times for breakfast otherwise I don't eat is much.Method. For a basic omelette, crack the eggs into a mixing bowl with a pinch of sea salt and black pepper. Beat well with a fork. Heat a small knob of butter in a small frying pan on a low heat, and once melted and bubbling, add the eggs and move the pan around to spread them out evenly.
